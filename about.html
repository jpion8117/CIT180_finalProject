My name is Josh Pion but everyone just calls me by my initials jP. My journey in software development began in fall of 2011 when I bought my first book on programming. I went to work immediately and began teaching myself the basics of the C++ programming language. For years, this was my on and off hobby. Then 2020 changed everything, working in a local grocery store, I was suddenly on the front lines in a global pandemic. I took my existing skills and learned enough javascript to hack together an automation script to keep track of some pandemic related things at work. This revitalized my waning interest in programming at the perfect time. Being a “frontline worker” in Michigan during the pandemic gave me a unique opportunity, with a newly created scholarship in my state I was finally given the chance to further my education in programming through college.
